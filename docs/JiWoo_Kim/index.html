About

Greetings! I'm a Master student at SungKyunKwan University in Human Language Intelligence Lab, and a wife of my amazing husband :) I'm particularly interested in how languages vary among different communties, as (online) communities grow in a world-scale. Inspired by this research and recent advance in large language models, now I am also curious on how user-centered design of AI product can change usability for online users, whose needs are various.

If you have any questions or would like to know about me XD, please contact me! I'm always happy to connect with others who share my interests! :D
