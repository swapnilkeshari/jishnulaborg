About Me

Hello! I am YeongJun Hwang, a Ph.D. candidate at Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU), where I am advised by Prof. JinYeong Bak in the Human Language Intelligence Lab.

My research focuses on culturally-adaptive and user-aware large language models (LLMs). I am particulary interested in how LLMs can infer user information-such as cultural backgrounds and demographic characteristics, and leverage this information to generate more contextually rich and personalized responses. To achieve this, I investigate how LLMs accuire and represent knowledge about diverse cultures and user attributes, and develop evaluation frameworks to assess these capabilities. My ultimate goal is to build personalized LLMs that understand diverse cultural contexts (e.g., natinality, generation) and individual user experiences, enabling more meaningful and tailored human-AI interactions.

  • Research Intern, SoftlyAI Inc, (Juy, 2023 - December 2023)
